Famous Olympians

Throughout USA swimming history, there have been numerous famous Olympians, who are noted for their accomplishments in swimming. Some of them are very well-known: Michael Phelps, for example. However, there are also those who are not as well-known, such as Lily King. To be considered an Olympian, you must medal at the Olympics, whether it be gold, silver, or bronze in an individual event or a relay. Noted below are two swimmers-one male and one female-who are Olympians.


Michael Phelps



Most likely the most known Olympic swimming athlete, Michael Phelps is recognized around the world. He is known as the "King of the Olympics" and is recognized worldwide. His best events include the 100M butterfly and the 200M butterfly. Below is a table of his major events and times:


Event Name Times
100 Meter Butterfly 49.82 seconds
200 Meter Butterfly 1:52.51



Rebecca Soni


Rebecca Soni was an Olympic swimmer who specialized in the 100M breaststroke and the 200M breaststroke. She is a six-time Olympian and was the first female swimmer to go under 2 minutes and 20 seconds in the 200M breaststroke. However, Rebecca Soni is now retired at the age of 31 and swims for fun and exercise rather than competiton. Below is a table of her major events and times:


Event Name Times
100 Meter Breaststroke 1:04.84
200 Meter Breaststroke 2:19.59
